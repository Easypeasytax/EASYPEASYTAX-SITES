📰 ITR Filing Due Date Extended

Deadline now September 15, 2025 — Know what it means for you

Published on: July 22, 2025

📢 The Income Tax Department has extended the ITR filing due date for non-audit taxpayers to September 15, 2025.

📌 Who’s Covered by This Extension?

  • Salaried individuals
  • Freelancers and small business owners (non-audit)
  • HUFs and individuals without audit requirement

📅 Updated ITR Deadlines (FY 2024–25)

  • Non-audit cases: September 15, 2025
  • Audit cases: October 31, 2025
  • Transfer pricing: November 30, 2025
  • Belated/Revised Return: December 31, 2025

🔍 Why Was It Extended?

  • Delays in issuing Form 16 and TDS updates
  • Taxpayer requests for more preparation time
  • Portal performance improvements underway

✅ What Should You Do?

  • Don’t wait till the last moment — file early
  • Use your Form 26AS, AIS, and Form 16 carefully
  • Consult a tax expert if your income includes business or capital gains

🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will late fees apply after September 15?
A: Yes. ₹1,000–₹5,000 under Section 234F.

Q: Can I revise my ITR after filing?
A: Yes. Revised returns are allowed up to December 31, 2025.
