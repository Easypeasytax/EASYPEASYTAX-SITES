Form 10-IEA: Switch to Old Tax Regime in AY 2025-26

What is Form 10-IEA?

Form 10-IEA is the declaration form that allows individuals with business or professional income to opt out of the New Tax Regime (which is the default from FY 2023-24 onwards) and choose the Old Tax Regime instead.

From AY 2025-26, the New Tax Regime is the default. If you want to opt for the Old Tax Regime and have business or professional income, you must file Form 10-IEA before the due date.

Who Must File Form 10-IEA?

CategoryRequirement
Individuals, HUFs, AOPs, BOIsMust have business/professional income
Filing ITR-3 or ITR-4Form 10-IEA required to opt Old Regime
Salaried Only (ITR-1/2)No need to file Form 10-IEA

Important Dates

  • Non-Audit Cases: 15 Sept 2025
  • Audit Cases: 31 Oct 2025

Steps to File Form 10-IEA Online

  1. Login to incometax.gov.in
  2. Navigate: e-File → Income Tax Forms → File Income Tax Forms
  3. Select "Form 10-IEA" and choose AY 2025-26
  4. Confirm your business income status and submit
  5. Verify using Aadhaar OTP, EVC, or DSC
  6. Save the acknowledgement number (ARN)
Note: Once you opt out of the New Regime (business income), re-entry is allowed only once in a lifetime.

When is Form 10-IEA Not Required?

If you're salaried only (no business income), you can directly choose the Old Tax Regime while filing ITR-1 or ITR-2 without filing Form 10-IEA.
