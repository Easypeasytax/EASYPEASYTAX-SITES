About EasyPeasyTax

We are a legal-tech initiative led by a practicing advocate helping startups, professionals, and businesses navigate India's tax and regulatory landscape. Our core expertise includes GST compliance, TDS filing, Income Tax responses, and business registrations including Trademark and MSME.

We are driven by the mission to simplify compliance for small businesses and professionals, making legal processes accessible and affordable. Our platform blends traditional legal expertise with modern digital tools to deliver prompt and reliable services.

What sets us apart is our personalized, compliance-first approach — ensuring that every filing, response, or registration is executed with precision and legal backing. We are not just service providers; we are legal custodians of your tax responsibilities.

Whether you're launching a startup, responding to a tax notice, or filing regular returns, we aim to be your dependable partner throughout the journey. Our support is proactive, ethical, and always rooted in current laws and updates.

Our goal is to ensure that entrepreneurs and professionals can focus on growth, while we handle the complexities of compliance in the background — securely, confidentially, and competently.
