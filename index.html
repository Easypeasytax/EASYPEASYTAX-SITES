About EasyPeasyTax

We are a legal-tech platform dedicated to simplifying tax and regulatory compliance in India. EasyPeasyTax supports startups, professionals, and businesses with services such as GST compliance, TDS filing, Income Tax notice responses, and business registrations including Trademark and MSME.

Our mission is to make legal and tax compliance simple, accessible, and affordable for individuals and growing businesses. By combining professional legal expertise with modern digital tools, we deliver efficient, reliable, and transparent compliance solutions.

What sets us apart is our compliance-first approach. Every filing, response, or registration is handled with accuracy, legal diligence, and attention to current regulations. We aim to provide not just services, but dependable tax and legal support you can trust.

Whether you are starting a new venture, responding to a tax notice, or managing ongoing GST and Income Tax compliance, EasyPeasyTax is designed to be your trusted partner.

Our goal is simple: to allow entrepreneurs and professionals to focus on growth while we manage the complexities of compliance — securely, confidentially, and competently.
