Why Linking Aadhaar with PAN is Important

  • Mandatory for filing ITR and most financial tasks.
  • PAN becomes inoperative if not linked; refunds and certain investments get blocked.
  • Prevents duplicate/fraudulent PANs and speeds up verification.

Penalty / Fee for Late Linking

PeriodFee
Until 31 Mar 2022₹0
1 Apr 2022 – 30 Jun 2022₹500
On/after 1 Jul 2022 (current)₹1,000

Note: If you’re linking today, you must pay ₹1,000 first via e-Pay Tax.

How to Link Aadhaar with PAN (Registered Users)

  1. Login: Go to incometax.gov.in → Login with PAN (User ID) and password.
  2. Open Link Aadhaar: Dashboard → Profile Settings → Link Aadhaar.
  3. Enter Aadhaar: Confirm PAN/Name/DOB; enter Aadhaar; tick consent and continue.
  4. Pay ₹1,000: You’ll be redirected to e-Pay Tax. Choose Current AY → Other Receipts (500) → pay via Net Banking/UPI/Debit Card.
  5. Submit after payment: Return to Link Aadhaar and submit again to send for UIDAI validation.

Heads-up: If you don’t see the payment recognized instantly, retry after a few minutes.

How to Link Aadhaar with PAN (Not Registered on Portal)

  1. Open pre-login page: Pre-login Link Aadhaar.
  2. Enter details: PAN, Aadhaar, Name, Mobile; tick consent to validate with UIDAI.
  3. Pay ₹1,000: You’ll be sent to e-Pay Tax (Any User). Select Current AY → Other Receipts (500) → complete payment.
  4. Submit linking: Go back to the same pre-login Link Aadhaar page and submit PAN + Aadhaar again.

No account needed: The entire process can be completed in pre-login mode.

Check Aadhaar–PAN Linking Status

Use the pre-login status page: Link Aadhaar Status → enter PAN & Aadhaar.

  • Linked: “Your PAN is linked with Aadhaar.”
  • Not Linked / Pending: Follow steps above and ensure fee is paid.
If you don’t link: PAN becomes inoperative; you can’t file ITR or receive refunds; higher TDS/TCS may apply.

FAQs

What is the current fee to link Aadhaar with PAN?
₹1,000 (for linkings requested on or after 1 July 2022).
Can I link without registering on the portal?
Yes. Use the pre-login Link Aadhaar service, pay ₹1,000 in e-Pay Tax (Any User), then submit the link request again.
What should I choose in e-Pay Tax?
Current Assessment Year and Other Receipts (500), then pay ₹1,000.
How long does it take?
Usually minutes. If status doesn’t update, recheck after a short while and resubmit the link request.
Do NRIs need to link?
Exemptions exist in specific cases (e.g., non-residents as per Income-tax Act). If unsure, consult a tax expert.
